The Will

The Will is a story about a young girl who inherited a wooden trunk filled with family belongings and an entire camera collection left to her by her grandfather.
She becomes interested in the cameras and how they function. After she developed the photos, an unexpected occurrence happened... the pictures were from the past, but who are these people?
She later learns that the people in the photos were her ancestors.
