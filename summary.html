“In 50-150 words, create a poem about a queen that smokes” ChatGPT, June 2, 2025, https://chat.openai.com.
The poem generated by ChatGPT inspired me to create artwork that explores the hidden vulnerabilities of a queen.
Often times we think of royalty as a powerful figure someone flawless or untouchable.

I was struck by the idea that even a queen is susceptible to the ordinary struggles of life.
The foundation of the artwork: portraying royal elegance with subtle signs of emotional burden, human flaws, and moral complexity.
I wanted to 'draw back the curtains' and reveal the illusion of perfection and elevate the truth that nobility does not exempt one from the human experience.
Even in leadership, strength and imperfection can coexist, even under a crown.


The following is the poem in its entirety:


In marble halls where shadows sprawl,
The queen sits still, beneath it all.
A cigarette between pale lips,
Her sorrow drips in slow eclipse.


Black crows perch on the window frame,
They caw her sins, they speak her name.
Their feathers gleam like onyx night— 
They watch her fall without a fight.


Her crown lies dull upon her hair,
A fading echo of despair.
The smoke curls up, a ghostly thread,
Of broken dreams and lovers fled.


She speaks to no one, barely breathes,
Just listens as the black crow grieves.
A court of ash, a throne of sighs,
With wingbeats cutting through her lies.


No diamonds flash, no scepter sways—
Just nicotine and darker days.
A queen undone, a world grown cold,
While black crows circle, brave and bold.
